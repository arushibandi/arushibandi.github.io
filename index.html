Hi! You have reached the severely under-developed Internet home page of myself, Arushi Bandi. Every year, I consider not renewing this domain because truly, I have not done much with it. But there have been a few bigger moments in its lifetime, so I keep it around for old times and potential future times sake. I am currently a software engineer / technologist living and working out of San Francisco.

Some of the things I've done on the more fun side have been designing & making the pieces for a fashion line considering how we may one day dress for (against) a surveillance society which received a microgrant from the Frank-Ratchye Studio for Creative Inquiry, teaching a class my senior year of college titled The Way We Live Now, which interrogated the relationship between technology and personhood, and participating in amazing classes from SFPC and Collective Action School.
