Back-to-School Smoothie!

August 30th, 2015

So, this is my first blog post. I'm not sure what exactly to blog about, but with school just starting, I couldn't resist sharing my new favorite breakfast smoothie. Honestly, I should probably be studying for my Scarlet Letter test instead of working on my website ...

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 Mango (the best fruit ever)
  • 1 handful of blueberries
  • 1 small frozen banana (if you don't have frozen bananas, just add ice!)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp hemp seeds
  • 1 tbsp protein powder
  • 2 tbsp Seven Nut and Seed Nuttzo butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
Instructions:
  1. Blend everything Together!
  2. Enjoy :)
